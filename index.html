About Me

I worked in the Termite Control field for over 20 years, starting out as a helper during my High School years and then eventually getting my own truck. It was a small company, and I was able to work my way all the way to running the day to day operations.

I have always been fascinated with coding, and would often go on github and just stare at various projects and wish I fully understood what was going on. I love android, and I root and mod every device I get. Currently own a Pixel XL and an old Nexus 7 (2013) that I tinker with when I have the time. Development of any kind is something I am very passionate about.

I married my best friend in November 2006 and we have 4 kids, three girls (8, 6, and 3) and one very, very spoiled boy (he is one). If I am not working or coding, I am spending time with my family.

At the end of August 2018 the owner of my company suddenly died with no will and no plan of succession for the company and all the employees where laid off. His plan was for me to step in and take over the business, so a sudden change of plans took us by surprise. My wife and I did some praying and soul-searching and I decided to follow my dreams and enroll in this Coding and Web Development Boot Camp. It was a challenge that lasted for 6 months, but I couldn't imagine life without it. Development grew from a side hobby to something I am truly passionate about, and I still wake up at 4 in the morning to make sure I can get in some practice every day.

My other technology interest is linux. I am especially a fan of Arch Linux, I love the simplicity of the base distro and the ability to install and configure however you need/want.
